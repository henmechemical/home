Company Profile

關於恒美About Henme

恒美化工股份有限公司創立於民國60年代初，適逢台灣紡織業與建築業蓬勃發展之際。公司積極引進日本尖端技術，與竹本油脂株式會社建立超過半世紀的深厚合作關係，共同開拓台灣高性能化學品市場。 Henme Chemical Co., Ltd. was established in the early 1970s, during a period of rapid growth in Taiwan's textile and construction industries. The company proactively introduced advanced Japanese technology and built a partnership with Takemoto Oil & Fat Co., Ltd. spanning over half a century, jointly developing the high-performance chemicals market in Taiwan.

目前設有台北總管理處及彰化鹿港彰濱工廠，業務範圍涵蓋生產製造、進出口貿易及技術代理，服務對象遍及台灣、越南及日本市場。 Today, we operate a headquarters in Taipei and a manufacturing plant in Changbin, Lukang, Changhua. Our business encompasses production, import/export trade, and technical agency services, with customers across Taiwan, Vietnam, and Japan.

Milestones

發展沿革Our History

1972

恒美化工成立Founded

由恒美貿易投資成立，開始生產纖維用助劑。Established by Henme Trading Co., commencing production of fiber finishing agents.

1975

日本技術合作Japan Technology Partnership

與竹本油脂株式會社締結技術合作，成立中日合資公司，設廠桃園龜山工業區。Established a technical collaboration with Takemoto Oil & Fat Co., Ltd. and formed a Sino-Japanese joint venture, building a plant in Guishan Industrial Zone, Taoyuan.

1993

擴展土木建築業務Expanded into Construction Chemicals

導入竹本油脂第3事業部技術，開始生產混凝土用添加劑。Introduced Division 3 technology from Takemoto, beginning production of concrete admixtures.

1999

彰濱工廠落成Changbin Plant Completed

彰化鹿港彰濱廠建廠完成，正式投產，龜山廠同年遷廠。The Changbin plant in Lukang, Changhua commenced full production; the Guishan plant was simultaneously relocated.

技術合作夥伴Our Partner

自1975年起，恒美與日本竹本油脂株式會社建立深度技術合作關係，歷經半世紀的共同成長，持續引進世界頂尖化工技術。 Since 1975, Henme has maintained a deep technical partnership with Takemoto Oil & Fat Co., Ltd. of Japan. Half a century of shared growth continues to bring world-class chemical technologies to Taiwan.
